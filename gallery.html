Edisan Trust

Edisan Trust founded in 2011, works to build equality and social justice by working with the most marginalized communities. The Founder of Edisan Trust who is rooted in bringing about social change by facilitating processes in which people from marginalized communities think critically, and take ownership of bringing about change. We believe that the most sustainable process of social change is a ‘bottom up’ approach of developing leadership capacities of the most marginalized.
