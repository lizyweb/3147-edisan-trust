About Us

A Synopsis of Edisan Trust

Edisan Trust founded in 2011, works to build equality and social justice by working with the most marginalized communities. The Founder of Edisan Trust who is rooted in bringing about social change by facilitating processes in which people from marginalized communities think critically, and take ownership of bringing about change. We believe that the most sustainable process of social change is a ‘bottom up’ approach of developing leadership capacities of the most marginalized.

Mission & Vision

Mission

Edisan Trust is committed to building equality and social justice by empowering the most marginalized communities. By facilitating critical thinking and fostering ownership among individuals, we strive to create sustainable social change. Our mission emphasizes the protection of women and child rights, health, education, and wellness for all.

Vision

To create an equitable society where marginalized communities lead the process of change, ensuring their voices are heard and their rights are upheld. We envision a world where every girl is empowered through education and awareness, communities thrive in health and self-sufficiency, and collective action fosters sustainable development for generations to come.

Women and Child Rights

Actively advocates for the protection and empowerment of women and children.

Livelihood Development

Encourages self-sustainability and economic independence through skill-building and support initiatives.

Community-Centric Approach

Believes in creating change through critical thinking and active participation of the communities themselves.

Women empowerment is the process of enhancing women's social, economic, political, and cultural strength to ensure they have equal rights and opportunities in all spheres of life.

Economic development is the sustained and inclusive growth of an economy that improves the standard of living, quality of life, and overall prosperity of a nation or community.

Awareness of government welfare schemes is crucial for ensuring that citizens, especially those from marginalized and underprivileged communities, can access the benefits designed to improve their quality of life.

A Self-Help Group (SHG) is a small, community-based group of individuals, often women, who come together to support one another in achieving financial independence, personal growth, and social empowerment.

Child Rights Awarness

On 26 July 2019 at Avinashi Block Avinashi Edisan trust conduct Child Rights Awareness training focuses on recognizing and responding to suspected risk of harm to children and young people resulting from parent or carer concerns. improved understanding of domestic and family violence and parent/carer substance misuse and its impacts and indicators.

Child Marriage Awarness

We work with communities to break down the inequitable systems and beliefs that force women and girls into marriage. At its core, forced child marriage is a fundamental violation of human rights. Every day around the world, women and girls are forced to marry against their will. On 13 July 2019 at Avinashi Block Avinashi Edisan trust organizes Child Marriage awareness and facilitates inclusion and participation of children works directly.

Women Empowerment Program

From the days of establishment of EDISAN TRUST, carries most of its activities in the community through Self Help Groups, which serve to be the backbone of all our projects. These SHGs are mainly trained on developing proper banking behavior and importance of small savings. Through this program they are also educated on book-keeping and chief functionary training.

Women Integrated Training

Edisan Trust has been working with Arunthathiyar women in Alathur, Avinashi taluk. On 27/09/2019 at Alathur Edisan Trust organize Integrated Training for Arunthathiyar Women. About 10 NGO heads from various districts to this training programme along with women.

Small Entrepreneur Development

Edisan Trust is the organization to have developed an entrepreneurship framework on integrate gender lens and identify barriers women face at each stage of becoming an entrepreneur. Thus, gender justice and training programmes are an integral part of our approach. The framework is a guiding tool in ascertaining five stages of journey.

Tailoring Training Program

Edisan Trust supporting for our noble cause of economically disadvantaged women. We emphasis mainly & quintessence on livelihood programs for women & girls to create sustainable livelihoods, build up confidence among Self Help Groups. started the self-employment training programs for poor women and girls & made a difference in many lives of women & their families.

Women Empowerment Through SHG

Women in the community form an SHG of 10-20 women from a similar occupation, residency, or economic condition. Edisan Trust mobilizes introduce members to the concept of the SHG which includes internal savings, loans, and repayment and mobilizes build the capacity of SHG leaders so that they are able to manage the SHG independently.

Women Living With Diginity

Edisan trust always gives respect to women and girls for their better and empowering them. Part of our on-going project we concentrate on women empowerment programs to create sustainable livelihoods, build up self confidence among Self Help Group Women Leaders.

Home Garden Awarness

Home gardens are found in many houses. They are sometimes called backyard or kitchen gardens. Gardening can enhance food security in several ways, most importantly through Direct access to a diversity of nutritionally-rich foods,Increased purchasing power from savings on food bills and income from sales of garden products, and fall-back food provision during seasonal lean periods.
